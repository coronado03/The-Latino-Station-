Empanadas

A savory pastry with a filling of meat, vegetables, or cheese.

Tacos

A traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn or wheat tortilla filled with various ingredients, such as meat, beans, vegetables, and cheese.

Ceviche

A seafood dish typically made from fresh raw fish cured in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime, and spiced with chili peppers.

Arepas

A type of bread made of ground maize dough or cooked flour prominent in the cuisine of Colombia and Venezuela.

Churrasco

A dish of grilled meat, typically beef, that is popular in many parts of Latin America.

Pupusas

A traditional Salvadoran dish made of a thick, handmade corn tortilla that is usually filled with a blend of cheese, cooked pork meat, refried beans, and/or loroco.
